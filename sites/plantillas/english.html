About COLFUTURO

The Foundation for the Future of Colombia, COLFUTURO, is a private non-profit organization created in 1991 by leaders from both the private and public sectors in Colombia to promote the formation of a critical mass of highly skilled and educated professionals, and thus support the country’s progress and competitive insertion into the global environment. This initiative was led by then First Lady, Ana Milena Muñoz de Gaviria.

COLFUTURO was created to provide the necessary financial and academic support for talented Colombian professionals to obtain a post-graduate education at some of the world’s top universities.

Based on the premise that both education and the development of human capital must be a shared responsibility, COLFUTURO received an initial endowment of US$ 12.7 million, of which US$ 7.2 million was contributed by the largest business groups in the country. The remaining US$ 5.5 million was provided by the government through the Secretariat of the President of the Republic, Ecopetrol (National Oil Company), the National Federation of Coffee Growers and the National Electricity Finance Company.

During its 18 years of operation, and without the need for significant additional donations, COLFUTURO’s fund has increased to nearly US$ 30 million. Throughout this period, and taking into account the funds that will be disbursed to the foundation has directly disbursed US$ 57.2 million and has converted US$14.8 million of debt into scholarships.

From its inception, COLFUTURO has supported 3,194 Colombian professionals, of whom 627 have completed Doctorate degrees and 2,567 have obtained Masters degrees.

The Loan-Scholarship Program

The Loan-Scholarship Program is COLFUTURO’s main program. It provides financial support for Colombian professionals pursuing postgraduate degrees overseas in any field of study.

Requirements

  • Colombian citizenship.
  • Completed undergraduate degree.
  • Letter of admission to a full-time postgraduate program in a university abroad.
  • The program must have a minimum duration of one academic year or nine months.
  • The loan is given for a program which is about to start or which may have already started.
  • Fluency in a second language, preferably English.

Characteristics

  • Loan-Scholarship is given in US dollars.
  • Maximum amount disbursed: US$ 25,000 per year or US$50,000 for two years.
  • A percentage of the loan becomes a scholarship when the beneficiary meets the following requirements: Completion of the program, returning to Colombia and staying in the country for the amount of time established by COLFUTURO.
  • Of the total amount disbursed, 50% becomes a scholarship. In the areas of Business and Administration, the conversion of debt into scholarship amounts to 25% of the loan.
  • An additional scholarship of 10% is granted to professionals who have a full-time job in the public sector, or with a research or academic institution.

Financial support from COLFUTURO may cover the following items

  • Airline tickets
  • Relocation expenses
  • Enrollment and tuition fees
  • Health insurance
  • Living expenses
  • Study materials, books and computer equipment

Call for submission of applications

The annual call for submission of applications to the Loan-Scholarship Program opens in mid-January and closes at the end of May each year. Applications are submitted online at www.colfuturo.org

Program for the Recruitment of Regional Talent

The Program for the Recruitment of Regional Talent was created to ensure a wider participation of Colombian professionals from regions outside of Bogotá in COLFUTURO’s Loan-Scholarship program.

The selection process for this program is highly rigorous, and focused on the same criteria for the selection of COLFUTURO’s Loan-Scholarship beneficiaries: the academic merit of candidates and the quality of the postgraduate program selected by the candidate.

The PRRT aims to reach outstanding students, alumni and faculty members of regional universities who are interested in pursuing postgraduate studies abroad.

Once candidates have been identified, the PRRT begins to interact closely with them to gain a deeper knowledge of their academic and professional strengths. The program provides advice and support to candidates throughout their process of research on postgraduate studies abroad, the preparation of their submission to postgraduate studies and their admission to some of the world’s best academic institutions.

COLFUTURO’s experience has demonstrated that professionals from regions outside of Bogotá are reluctant to participate in the Loan-Scholarship selection process either because they lack foreign language skills, they do not have enough knowledge on how to select graduate programs best suited to their needs and interests, or because they believe that they cannot cover the costs of living and studying overseas.

COLFUTURO aims to strengthen its focus in the areas of engineering, health sciences, basic sciences, economics, social sciences and education through the PRRT.

By creating this program, COLFUTURO aims to strengthen its social, economic, and multiplier impact by increasing the participation of students from other regions and public universities who possess huge potential to contribute to Colombia’s regional development and international growth.

The PRRT was launched in 2006 and currently has 870 participants. In 2008, seven program participantes were selected as COLFUTURO beneficiaries; in 2009, the number of selected participants increased to 20. A total of 34 PRRT participants have been admitted to foreign universities without funding from COLFUTURO. These students have been granted scholarships from agencies such as the World Bank, Colombia’s Central Bank, the Fulbright Commission and research centers located abroad.

The Program for the Recruitment of Regional Talent was created to ensure a wider participation of Colombian professionals from regions outside of Bogotá in COLFUTURO’s Loan-Scholarship program.

Academic Advising Program

COLFUTURO’s Academic Advising Program offers free, personalized information and counseling on postgraduate international education and studying abroad. It fulfills its objective by:

  1. Providing advice and orientation to professionals on how to select high quality postgraduate programs that best meet their individual academic and professional interests
  2. Permanently updating the information in COLFUTURO’s webpage on postgraduate studies abroad
  3. Compiling independent analyses which classify and rank postgraduate programs offered by universities abroad, in order to assess and compare programs and academic institutions during COLFUTURO’s selection process each year.
  4. Promoting opportunities for postgraduate studies abroad and disseminating information on COLFUTURO in different cities of Colombia.

The Academic Advising Program is also responsible for participating in educational fairs, hosting open houses with partner universities and promoting COLFUTURO’s mission and work among universities abroad.
